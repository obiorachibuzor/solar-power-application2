Ten(10) Minutes Interval Solar Energy Forecasting


Steps to Use this App:

1. Enter the current actual solar radiation value from your GHI sensor

2. After 10 minutes enter the value for the 2nd interval from your sensor

3. After another 10 minutes enter the value for the 3rd interval from your sensor

4. Enter the capacity of one solar panel installed

5. Enter the number of solar panels installed

6. Enter the area of one solar panel installed

7. Click on the button to predict solar energy at ten minutes inteval
